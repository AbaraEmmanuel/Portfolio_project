Track your expenses effortlessly with Spendwise - A user-friendly expense tracker application.

Features

Expense Tracking

Record and manage your expenses conveniently. Enter item names, prices, and purchase dates to keep track of your spending habits easily. Spendwise helps you stay organized by categorizing your expenditures and providing insightful data analysis.

Budget Management

Set monthly budgets and monitor your spending against these limits. Receive alerts when you're approaching or exceeding your budget, enabling you to make informed financial decisions. With Spendwise, you can take control of your finances and plan for a better financial future.

User-Friendly Interface

Enjoy a sleek and intuitive interface designed for seamless user experience. Spendwise offers simple navigation and clear visualizations, making it easy for users of all levels to manage their expenses efficiently. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced budgeter, Spendwise provides a hassle-free way to track your finances.

About

personal expense tracking and budget management. Recognizing the importance of financial organization in daily life, the project was born out of a need to simplify the process of monitoring expenses and staying within budgetary constraints. The idea was to develop a tool that not only allows users to record their expenditures but also provides insights into their spending habits, helping them make informed financial decisions. By combining simplicity with functionality, Spendwise aims to empower individuals to take control of their finances and achieve their financial goals. Moreover, Spendwise serves as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, showcasing the skills and expertise gained through the educational journey while demonstrating real-world application in addressing practical challenges. Through this project, the team endeavors to contribute to the development of innovative solutions and foster a culture of continuous learning and improvement.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School. You can find the repository here.
